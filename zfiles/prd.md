📄 PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
BYWI Website (Brighter Youth and Women Initiative)
1. Overview

The BYWI website is a public-facing platform designed to:

Communicate the organization’s mission and impact
Build trust with donors, partners, and the public
Enable engagement through donations, volunteering, and partnerships

The website will be built using Next.js with a headless CMS (Sanity) and optional backend services (Supabase) for data-driven features.

2. Objectives
Primary Goals
Clearly communicate BYWI’s mission within 5 seconds
Increase visibility and credibility
Enable donation and engagement
Secondary Goals
Showcase programs and impact
Provide a platform for updates (blog/news)
Enable internal data tracking (future phase)
3. Target Audience
